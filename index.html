Services

As of this moment, these are the services I offer

Web Designing

Web designing refers to the process of creating and designing the visual elements, layout, and overall aesthetic of a website. It involves a combination of graphic design, user interface (UI) design, and user experience (UX) design to produce a visually appealing, functional, and user-friendly website. Web designers use various tools and technologies to bring a website to life, ensuring it meets the needs and expectations of its intended audience.

System Developing

System development, also known as systems development or system engineering, is a process that involves designing, creating, testing, and maintaining information systems. These systems can range from simple software applications to complex enterprise-wide solutions that integrate hardware, software, networks, and people. The goal of system development is to create efficient and effective solutions that meet the needs of organizations or individuals.

Video Editing

Video editing is the process of manipulating and rearranging video shots to create a new and cohesive work. It involves cutting and arranging video clips, adding transitions, visual effects, audio elements, and other enhancements to produce a finished video product. Video editing is a crucial part of the post-production process in filmmaking, television production, and content creation for various digital platforms.

Photo Editing

Photo editing refers to the process of modifying or enhancing digital or printed photographs using various techniques and software tools. The goal of photo editing is to improve the overall quality, composition, and visual appeal of an image. This can involve adjusting colors, contrast, brightness, and sharpness, as well as removing unwanted elements or adding creative effects.

Project 1

ATM System

An ATM system, or Automated Teller Machine system, refers to the combination of hardware, software, and communication technologies that enable the operation of ATMs. ATMs are electronic devices that provide financial services, primarily allowing users to perform basic banking transactions without the need for human assistance. Common transactions include withdrawing cash, checking account balances, transferring funds between accounts, and sometimes depositing cash or checks.

Project 2

BJMP Visitors Log Monitoring System (Prototype)

A Visitors Log Monitoring System is a software-based solution designed to track and manage the entry and exit of visitors in a specific location, such as an office building, facility, or event venue. This system aims to enhance security, streamline visitor registration processes, and maintain a digital record of visitor activity for various purposes, including security, compliance, and analysis.

Ipil Tour Online Accomodation Booking System

An online accommodation booking system is a web-based platform that allows users to search, compare, and book accommodations such as hotels, resorts, hostels, vacation rentals, and more through the internet. These systems provide a convenient and efficient way for travelers to plan their stays, and for accommodation providers to manage bookings and availability. Online accommodation booking systems have become a standard and popular method for both leisure and business travelers to secure lodging arrangements.
